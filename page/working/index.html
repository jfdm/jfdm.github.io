Working with me

Currently, I have no funded positions available for research associates and assistants.

When I do have project related positions available, they will be advertised through the normal channels: social media; and on jobs.ac.uk

Another approach is to jointly apply for funding for a bespoke position. Example, funding sources are: Marie Curie Actions; Royal Academy of Engineering; and Royal Commission 1851. If you are interested then please do contact me so that we can chat about it before you submit your application. I would be grateful if you can mention in your initial email why my work interests you, and detail any ideas you have that align with my research interests. If I feel that we are a good match I will work with you to develop your application.
